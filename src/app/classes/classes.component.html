Business Class


Taking AirFuselage hospitality to the skies! In Business Class, we make your trip the most pleasurable experience, with award winning dishes,
the latest in-flight entertainment system, and comfortable seating. By choosing Business Class, you too can feel special in the skies and
travel in comfort as you enjoy the miles you have earned through Miles&Smiles.

Maximum comfort

Our Business Class seating has been specially designed for your comfort to the highest level. We double your joy of travel with special features such as in-chair massage, reading lamp, power supply, and the facility to screen off your own private area.

Extra baggage allowance

Traveling in Business Class entitles you to extra baggage allowance and also means you can collect your baggage much more quickly after the flight, thanks to your Business Class priority baggage label.

Inflight Catering

Throughout your comfortable journey in Business Class, our flying chefs will present you with the finest examples of Indian and world cuisine, we make sure that your trip is also a journey of tastes, with our award-winning dining on board service, prepared by Indian Do&Co.

Preferential check-in

Passengers who choose Business Class can avoid airport queues by checking in at one of our dedicated Business Class counters. Fast check-in process is delivered to aur business class passengers by checking in at one of our special Business Class desks at the airport, So that you can avoid large queues.
